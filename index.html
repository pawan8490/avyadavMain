My Breakthrough Work

2005
Reducing industrial air pollution

Introduced CEM technologies (continuous emission monitoring) & dust monitors for the first time across India to over 200 cement plants. These real-time technologies provided the foundation for reducing industrial emissions, and hence positively impacted the air pollution levels.

2011
Creating world's first health band for Animals

Innovated world's first wearable animal health-monitoring band that tracks key vital signs and wellness indicators. It was named one of the best new wearable tech health and wellness devices at CES 2014.

2016
Making eCommerce solutions for MSMEs

Created an online eCommerce platform for MSMEs that buy & sell industrial products across India. First time features like micro enterprise-portals expanded business for MSMEs in far corners, and thus new job opportunities got created for people. State government used the platform to support & benefit MSMEs.

2016
Fostering rural prosperity & preserving culture

An initiative to foster rural prosperity, and to curb the forced migration of villagers to cities. My team is committed to create new economic opportunities at the village level, and most importantly, help preserve our sanatan culture, joint-family system & values. Mission Swarna Gram, Guru Tula-Takshila and Vigyanta are such leading initiatives to achieve the overall objectives. We aim to build a vibrant and self-reliant Bharat rooted in timeless traditions and modern growth.
